Portfolio Website for Furniture/Architectural Designer.

Worked closely with client in order to create a showcase for his work.

Created in close co-operation with Gleeson that, as a designer himself, had lots of ideas and suggestions for the look and feel of his portfolio. Working from my prototypes we iterated many times over to reach a point we both felt happy with. A lot of focus on details.

Single page application implemented with Node. Mostly jQuery and vanilla js. Used gulp for creating a smooth workflow.
