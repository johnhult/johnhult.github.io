About me

So, let me tell you a little bit about myself.

Frontend Developer with an eye for design and UX/UI. Studies Software Engineering at Chalmers University. Strolling the streets of Gothenburg in wait for the zombie apocalypse.

History

Born and raised outside the lovely city of Gothenburg, I found my love for computer-/videogames early on. Obviously, I was going to work with games. Or so I thought. But life took some turns and I ended up studying theatre/drama in high school. I applied for a university in the field but didn't get in. Following this, I started working as a bartender for a year but realized I wanted to do something else. I attended a engineering preparatory year at Chalmers and later on started on Software Engineering, finding my way back to my dream of making games. However, I found that actually making games wasn't my cup of tea and instead nurtured my love for design and focused more on frontend development. And that pretty much sums it up.
